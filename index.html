What We Offer

Treatments Tailored to You

Every skin is unique. Our services are designed to work with your skin's individual needs — addressing aging, dehydration, sensitivity, acne, and more.

✦

Hydro Facial

A deeply hydrating multi-step treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, and nourishes with serums tailored to your skin. Leaves you with an immediate, luminous glow.

✦

Custom Facial

A bespoke facial designed around your skin's current needs. Whether addressing dryness, congestion, sensitivity, or aging — every step is chosen with intention for your skin.

✦

Dermaplaning

A precise manual exfoliation technique that removes dead skin cells and fine vellus hair, leaving skin remarkably smooth, radiant, and primed for deeper product absorption.

✦

Lash Lift & Tint

A semi-permanent treatment that lifts, curls, and defines your natural lashes with a rich tint — delivering the look of mascara with zero daily effort.

✦

Microneedling

Clinically proven collagen induction therapy that stimulates your skin's natural repair process. Targets fine lines, texture, acne scarring, and loss of firmness.

✦

Waxing Services

Smooth, precise waxing for face and body using high-quality formulas that are gentle on the skin and effective on even the most stubborn fine hair.

✦

Mommy & Me Facial

A gentle, bonding skincare experience for mothers and daughters. Both treatments are fully customized — a perfect shared ritual or meaningful gift for any occasion.

Meet Amy

Your skin deserves an expert who truly cares.

Amy Rowe is a licensed esthetician certified in microneedling, dermaplaning, and chemical peels. With a background as a Surgical Center Administrator and Surgical Scrub Technician, she brings a clinical precision and attention to detail that sets her work apart.

Amy specializes in personalized treatments for aging, dehydrated, sensitive, and acne-prone skin — combining advanced technique with a warm, welcoming touch. Whether you're targeting a specific concern or simply want to invest in your skin, you're in expert hands.
